📌 Overview

This document outlines the end-to-end workflow for onboarding new clients onto the company's service platform. The goal is to ensure every client receives a consistent, professional, and timely onboarding experience.

Estimated Completion Time: 5–7 business days
Teams Involved: Client Services, IT Support, Contracts, Finance

🔄 Workflow Stages

Stage 1: Initial Client Intake

Owner: Client Services Coordinator
Timeline: Day 1

  • Receive new client referral from Sales (HubSpot)
  • Create client record in HubSpot
  • Send welcome email within 24 hours
  • Schedule onboarding call (Outlook/Teams)
  • Create SharePoint folder

Checklist:

  • ✔ Client record created
  • ✔ Welcome email sent
  • ✔ Call scheduled
  • ✔ SharePoint folder created

Stage 2: Requirements Gathering

  • Conduct onboarding call
  • Document requirements
  • Confirm SLA and communication channels
  • Share onboarding checklist
  • Log notes in HubSpot

Key Questions:

  • What services are you subscribing to?
  • Who are your main contacts?
  • Reporting preference?
  • Existing contracts?

Stage 3: Contract Review & Execution

  • Send contract via DocuSign
  • Follow up within 48 hours
  • Save signed contract in SharePoint
  • Update HubSpot status
  • Notify Finance

Stage 4: System Setup

  • Submit IT request (ServiceNow)
  • Provision access (Portal, Teams, SharePoint)
  • Send credentials securely
  • Confirm login success

Stage 5: Onboarding Completion

  • Schedule confirmation call
  • Walk through portal
  • Share handbook
  • Mark onboarding complete
  • Set 30-day follow-up
