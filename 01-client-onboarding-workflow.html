# 📋 Client Onboarding Coordination Workflow **Document Type:** Client Services Workflow **Category:** Client Coordination **Version:** 1.2 **Last Updated:** March 2025 **Author:** Margaret Muriuki, Client Services Coordinator --- ## 📌 Overview This document outlines the end-to-end workflow for onboarding new clients onto the company's service platform. The goal is to ensure every client receives a consistent, professional, and timely onboarding experience. **Estimated Completion Time:** 5–7 business days **Teams Involved:** Client Services, IT Support, Contracts, Finance --- ## 🔄 Workflow Stages ### Stage 1: Initial Client Intake **Owner:** Client Services Coordinator **Timeline:** Day 1 **Actions:** - Receive new client referral from the Sales team via email or CRM (HubSpot) - Create a new client record in HubSpot with all relevant contact details - Send a welcome email to the client within 24 hours of referral receipt - Schedule a 30-minute onboarding call using Microsoft Outlook/Teams - Create a shared folder in SharePoint: `Clients > [Client Name] > Onboarding` **Checklist:** - [ ] Client record created in HubSpot - [ ] Welcome email sent - [ ] Onboarding call scheduled - [ ] SharePoint folder created --- ### Stage 2: Requirements Gathering **Owner:** Client Services Coordinator **Timeline:** Day 2–3 **Actions:** - Conduct onboarding call with the client - Document client requirements using the standard intake form - Confirm service tier, SLA expectations, and preferred communication channels - Share onboarding checklist with the client via email - Log all meeting notes in HubSpot under the client record **Key Questions to Ask:** 1. What services are you subscribing to? 2. Who are your primary contacts for day-to-day communication? 3. What are your reporting preferences (weekly, monthly)? 4. Do you have any existing contracts or agreements to review? **Documents Required from Client:** - Signed service agreement - Company registration documents (if applicable) - Primary contact information sheet --- ### Stage 3: Contract Review & Execution **Owner:** Client Services Coordinator + Contracts Team **Timeline:** Day 3–4 **Actions:** - Send the service contract to the client via DocuSign - Follow up within 48 hours if contract has not been signed - Once signed, save the executed contract to: `SharePoint > Clients > [Client Name] > Contracts` - Update HubSpot: mark contract status as **"Executed"** - Notify Finance team to set up client billing profile **Escalation:** If contract is not returned within 5 business days, escalate to Account Manager. --- ### Stage 4: System Setup & Access Provisioning **Owner:** IT Support Team (coordinated by Client Services) **Timeline:** Day 4–5 **Actions:** - Submit IT access request ticket via ServiceNow - Confirm the following are provisioned: - Client portal access - Microsoft Teams shared channel (if applicable) - SharePoint document sharing permissions - Send the client their login credentials securely - Confirm client has successfully logged in **IT Request Template (ServiceNow):** > **Request Type:** New Client Access Setup > **Client Name:** [Name] > **Services Required:** [List services] > **Requested By:** [Your Name] > **Priority:** Medium > **Due Date:** [Date] --- ### Stage 5: Onboarding Confirmation & Handover **Owner:** Client Services Coordinator **Timeline:** Day 5–7 **Actions:** - Schedule a 15-minute onboarding confirmation call - Walk the client through the portal and available resources - Share the Client Services Handbook (PDF) via email - Update HubSpot: mark onboarding status as **"Complete"** - Set a follow-up reminder in HubSpot for 30 days post-onboarding - Notify Account Manager that onboarding is complete **Closing Email Template:** > Dear [Client Name], > > Thank you for completing your onboarding with us. Your account is now fully active. > Your dedicated contact is [Name] reachable at [email]. > Please do not hesitate to reach out if you need any assistance. > > Warm regards, > Margaret Muriuki > Client Services Coordinator --- ## 📊 Onboarding Tracker (Sample) | Client Name | Intake Date | Contract Status | Access Provisioned | Onboarding Complete | |----------------------|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|----------------------| | ISON Global | 03 Mar 2025 | ✅ Executed | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | | Spectlance Global | 10 Mar 2025 | ⏳ Pending | ❌ No | ❌ No | | ISON Global | 18 Mar 2025 | ✅ Executed | ✅ Yes | ⏳ In Progress | --- ## 🚨 Escalation Path | Issue | Escalate To | Timeline | |-------|-------------|----------| | Client unresponsive for 3+ days | Account Manager | Immediately | | Contract not signed after 5 days | Account Manager | Day 5 | | IT access not provisioned after 2 days | IT Support Lead | Day 2 | | Billing setup delayed | Finance Manager | Day 3 | --- ## 🛠️ Tools Used | Tool | Purpose | |------|---------| | HubSpot CRM | Client records, follow-up tracking | | Microsoft Outlook | Email communication, scheduling | | Microsoft Teams | Client calls, internal coordination | | SharePoint | Document storage and sharing | | ServiceNow | IT access request tickets | | DocuSign | Contract execution | --- *Document maintained by the Client Services team.
